Los Angeles Metro Bike Share Analysis

Welcome! Scroll down to view an in depth Metro Bike Share analysis!

What is the Average Distance Traveled?

The average distance traveled from 2016 to 2017 was 403.70 miles. The most popular month was February 2017.
Bikers in total traveled an average of 465.25 miles and 1806.90 seconds! February was definitely a busy month
for Metro Bike Share. The weather must have been wonderful in Los Angeles.
To view other averages, hover over the bars!

Which Bike Share Stop is the Most Popular?

Stop 3082 (Traction & Rose) is the most popular stop.Other popular stops are 3014 (Union Station West Portal),
3069 (Broadway & 3rd), 3031 (7th & Spring), and 3064 (Grand & 7th). Each of the stops received 2000 to 3000
visitors from 2016 to 2017. Wow!

How Does Ridership Change with Seasons?

During the Winter, regular commuters decline from approximately 10,000 riders to 6000 riders—most likely because
of the cold weather. On the contrary, Bike Share is popular during Summer months. Regarding trip route/passholder types,
Monthly and Flex passes are popular during warmer months and not popular during colder months as well!

What Might Make a Particular Trip Route/Passholder Type Popular?

Considering the popularity of One Way routes and Monthly Passes, their combination is the most popular among riders.
Perhaps, this is the most popular because as a regular commuter, an individual would use Bike Share
to commute to work directly instead of bike back to his or her starting stop!
